Rigorous air audit protocol

Strict Cleanroom Validation Testing

Before we hand over any cleanroom setup, we execute multiple validations verifying that structural joins are hermetically sealed and laminar flows comply with safety specifications.

We perform filter integrity tests (PAO/DOP), room pressure differential measurements, particle concentration counts, and airflow visualization trials, delivering a complete ISO compliance validation document with every installation.
