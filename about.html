About Prime Air Lab Solutions

Prime Air Lab Solutions is a trusted engineering and infrastructure company specializing in cleanroom systems, modular operation theatres, pharmaceutical laboratory infrastructure, scientific doors, fire-rated systems, laboratory furniture, and controlled environment solutions.

We deliver customized, high-quality solutions for pharmaceutical industries, hospitals, laboratories, biotechnology facilities, and industrial sectors while maintaining strict standards for quality, hygiene, and safety.
